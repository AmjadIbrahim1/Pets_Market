Popular Pets in Egypt

Birds are intelligent, social, and interactive pets that bring joy with their singing and vibrant personalities. Popular pet birds include parrots, budgies, canaries, and cockatiels. They require a spacious cage, mental stimulation, and a proper diet consisting of seeds, fruits, and vegetables. Birds thrive on companionship and need daily interaction with their owners to stay happy and healthy.

Horses are majestic and loyal companions that require a significant amount of care, space, and commitment. They need a proper stable, a nutritious diet of hay and grains, and regular exercise. Grooming, vet check-ups, and hoof care are essential for their well-being. While horses require more responsibility than other pets, they offer strong bonds and rewarding experiences, especially for equestrians.

Turtles are low-maintenance and fascinating pets that can live for decades. They require a well-maintained tank with clean water, UV lighting, and a balanced diet of leafy greens, insects, and protein. Different species have varying habitat needs‚Äîsome prefer aquatic environments, while others thrive in dry enclosures. With proper care, turtles can be wonderful lifelong companions.
